# Privacy Policy for TabTidy

**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

## Overview

TabTidy ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how TabTidy handles your data.

## Data Collection

**TabTidy does NOT collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.**

### What We Access

TabTidy requires the following Chrome permissions to function:

- **tabs:** To read and manage your open tabs (titles, URLs, favicons)
- **storage:** To save your tab groups locally on your device
- **tabGroups:** To create and manage Chrome native tab groups
- **sessions:** To access recently closed tabs for the history feature

### Where Your Data is Stored

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's local storage API. Specifically:

- **Saved tab groups:** Stored in `chrome.storage.local`
- **Settings preferences:** Stored in `chrome.storage.local`
- **Hidden history IDs:** Stored in `chrome.storage.local`

**We do not have access to any of this data.** It never leaves your device.

## What We Do NOT Do

- ❌ We do NOT collect any personal information
- ❌ We do NOT track your browsing history
- ❌ We do NOT send your data to any external servers
- ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- ❌ We do NOT share your data with third parties
- ❌ We do NOT display advertisements

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in security
- Only you have access to your saved tab groups and settings
- Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **View:** All saved data is visible within the extension
- **Delete:** You can delete any saved groups at any time
- **Clear:** You can clear all data by uninstalling the extension

## Third-Party Services

TabTidy does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.
